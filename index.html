Welcome to My Website!

Hi, my name is Jason Lo and I am a student at Northeastern University. I am studying business administration and have a deep interest in finance. This website is a place for me to share a little about who I am, what I enjoy, and what I have been working on.

I'm a proud Bay Area native, born and raised in Northern California with deep roots in the Asian American community there. Growing up in the Bay Area shaped everything about me from my love of good food, to my appreciation for diverse cultures, and my drive to pursue ambitious goals. I have a wide range of interests, from cooking and exploring new cuisines, to following the stock market, to competing in card and board games with friends. I'm also a huge fan of classic Nintendo games whether it be a fun round of golf in Wii Sports or a heated race in Mario Kart. When I'm not indoors, you'll find me outside. I'm an avid outdoors person who has gone mountaineering in Alaska, hit the slopes at Lake Tahoe, and backpacked through trails across the Bay Area. Traveling is another big passion of mine, and I love experiencing new places, people, and food whenever I get the chance.

Professionally, I have been fortunate to build experience across sports, finance, and strategy consulting. I've done consulting, strategy work, and corporate finance across organizations such as the NBA, the Golden State Warriors, Brown Brothers Harriman, and Denneen and Company. This coming September, I'm excited to be joining EY-Parthenon as a decision modeling and analytics associate, where I look forward to tackling complex business challenges and continuing to grow as a strategist and problem solver.
