My Interests & Hobbies

One of my greatest passions in life is travel. My love for exploring the world started at a young age, with family trips to classic California destinations like Disneyland, Los Angeles, San Diego, and Yosemite. Those early adventures sparked a genuine curiosity about the world beyond my own backyard. It wasn't long before those road trips evolved into flights, and my first international trip to Mexico opened my eyes to just how much there is to see and experience outside of the United States.

Since then, I've been fortunate enough to travel extensively across Asia, Europe, and the Americas. My passport has taken me to Japan, Hong Kong, Singapore, Taiwan, Costa Rica, Panama, Dominican Republic, France, Italy, Spain, Germany, Czech Republic and Mexico. Closer to home, I've explored New York City, Las Vegas, Hawaii, and the breathtaking landscapes of numerous national parks. If I had to pick a favorite, it would be a tough choice between Hong Kong and Japan since both cities completely captivated me with their incredible food scenes, rich culture, distinctive fashion, and unique energy that you simply can't find anywhere else. Furthermore, my family is from Hong Kong, so witnessing the circumstances they grew up made me develop a new appreciation.

What I love most about traveling is the way it broadens your perspective. Experiencing different cultures, cuisines, customs, and ways of life makes you a more open-minded and well-rounded person. I especially enjoy exploring local markets, trying street food, and hunting for unique fashion and goods. To me, shopping is absolutely part of the travel experience for me! Whether I'm with close friends or family, every trip creates memories that last a lifetime. Looking ahead, I'm thrilled to be heading to Vietnam and South Korea this summer, and I have my sights set on Iceland and Spain or Portugal in the near future. The list keeps growing, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

My Travel Bucket List

  • Explore Cape Town, South Africa
  • Hike Mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania
  • Watch the Northern Lights in Iceland

  • Go on a bush plane hunting trip in the Alaskan wilderness
  • Visit Vietnam and South Korea (coming this summer!)
